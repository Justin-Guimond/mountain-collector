About the Mountain Collector

Justin is on a mission to hike New England's 67 4,000 footers. In other words, there are 67 mountains in New England with a peak elevation of at least 4,000 feet. In 2017, Justin completed a large chunk of these when hiking 700 miles from Maine to Connecticut on the Appalachian Trail. Stay tuned for the latest mountain collection. Happy Trails!
