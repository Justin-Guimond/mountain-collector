About the Mountain Collector

Justin is on a mission to hike New England's 67 4,000 footers. Let me explain. There are 67 mountains in New England with a peak elevation of at least 4,000 feet and Justin is trying to reach the summit of all of them. In 2017, he completed a large chunk of these when hiking 700 miles from Maine to Connecticut on the Appalachian Trail. Stay tuned for the latest mountain collection. Happy Trails!
